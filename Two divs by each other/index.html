What Social Media Is

Social Media It is the most modern tool on the internet
that keeps you informed on any type of topic.

You can have your own profile in which you can add
all the information you want about yourself.

In social media you can find many pictures of a person in a single profile.
information about the person, like where the person study, works, what the person like,
and a lot more just with one click.

An important part of marketing today are social media.You can make your business more popular with the simple fact of having
a social account for your business and a great advantage of this
is that you can have all kinds of spectators.

Not only can you have your business on social Media but you can also make the purchases you want there.
It has its advantages and disadvantages but it gives you the opportunity to find and buy what you want with more
accessibility and without the need to leave home.

Now with social media, communicating is not a problem!
You can communicate with someone being next to that person as being 100km away or in a different country.
Now wherever you are you can let people know what you want and what you think ...
but keep an eye on this because you never know who is on the other side and you have to be careful with what we share in the networks.

The whole world has different types of social media and we can use them in different ways as we have seen.
We must take advantage of the opportunity we have to have this tools and use them wisely and for our benefit.
But as these tools we as developers can improve them or create new ones with better options and more advantages.
